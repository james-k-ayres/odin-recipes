Beef and Cheese Empanadas

You leftovers will not last long, (if you even have them). Behold! the empanada, delicious little edible vehicle of flavor from some really wonderful cultura

Ingredients

  • empanada discos (5" discs)
  • ground beef
  • sliced cheese
  • Salsa
  • Frying Oil
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper, oregano, garlic)
  • Orange Marinade for the meat
  1. Marinade the ground beef at least 24 hours before cooking
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute the beef, cooking until browned, about 10 minutes. Remove the beef with a slotted spoon and set aside.
  3. Get your discs ready, slightly defrosted. Once your dough has been rolled out, place 2 heaping tablespoons of filling in the center of each dough circle. Add ½ teaspoon salsa to each circle, and add half a slice of cheese, if using. Brush a small amount of egg wash lightly over one half edge’s border. Fold over the dough to create a half-moon shape and press edges firmly to seal. Crimp edges with the back of a fork.
  4. Place the empanadas in vegetable or frying oil and cook, (oil is approx 350 degrees). Cook the empanadas until golden brown.
  5. Let cool, and then dig in!
