Chicken Fried Rice

Bring the restaurant style home with this Better than take out Chicken Fried Rice. Best part: one skillet, twenty minutes. Rice, Veggies, and Protein with a deliciious flavor. Let's get into it!

Ingredients

  • Cooking Oil (Vegertable, Olive, Peanut)
  • Sesame Oil
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Frozen Peas and Carrots
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • White Rice (or Brown)
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt and Pepper

Cooking Instructions

  1. Add the oils and diced chicken to a large skillet (or wok, if youâ€™re lucky enough to own one!). Cook over medium-high heat for about 5 minutes, or until the chicken is no longer pink inside. Remove the chicken to a plate while you stir-fry the veggies.
  2. To the same skillet, add the peas, carrots, and green onions. Once softened, add the garlic.
  3. Push the veggies to one side of the skillet, and scramble the eggs on the other side.
  4. Add the chicken back to the pan, followed by the cooked rice.
  5. Drizzle everything with soy sauce and cook until everything is heated through.
  6. Dig in!
